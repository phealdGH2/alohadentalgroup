Meet the Doctor

Dr. Farah Bennett

"Combining Clinical Excellence with the Art of Aesthetic Design."

Dr. Farah Bennett is a highly skilled general dentist dedicated to providing world-class care with a gentle, patient-centered approach. A graduate of the prestigious University at Buffalo School of Dental Medicine, she brings a foundation of elite clinical training to the Aloha Dental Group team.

While Dr. Bennett excels in all aspects of foundational dentistry, she has a particular passion for Advanced Aesthetic Transformations. She believes that a healthy smile is a work of art, and she utilizes the latest dental innovations to help her patients achieve results that are both functional and beautiful.

Fotona Laser Specialist

Dr. Bennett specializes in non-invasive laser treatments, including NightLase® for sleep breathing and facial aesthetics for natural rejuvenation.

Invisalign Expert

Leveraging Diamond-level clear aligner technology to create perfectly aligned smiles without the need for traditional metal braces.

Dr. Bennett understands that for many, a visit to the dentist can be stressful. She is known for her calm demeanor and "gentle touch," ensuring that every patient feels relaxed—bringing the true spirit of Aloha to the San Mateo community.
