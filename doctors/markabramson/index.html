Dr. Mark Abramson

Dr. Mark Abramson is a world-renowned specialist in the fields of TMJ therapy, Craniofacial Pain, and Dental Sleep Medicine. His presence at Aloha Dental Group provides our clinical team with direct access to decades of pioneering research and diagnostic expertise.

Clinical Innovation

Dr. Abramson is the developer of the OASYS Oral Lift™, the first dental device to gain FDA approval for treating both snoring and sleep apnea by specifically addressing the nasal airway.

As a Diplomate of the American Board of Dental Sleep Medicine and the American Academy of Craniofacial Pain, Dr. Abramson has spent his career developing non-invasive, therapeutic solutions for complex pain and sleep-disordered breathing.

At Aloha Dental Group, Dr. Abramson serves in a specialist support role, ensuring that our patients benefit from the most advanced diagnostic protocols and therapeutic technologies available in modern dental medicine today.
