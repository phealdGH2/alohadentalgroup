Dr. Terry Codington

Why I like being a Dentist

"I love empowering my patients and giving them smiles they are proud of. Dentistry gives me the chance to be creative by using both my scientific and artistic talents. I thoroughly enjoy implementing the advancement of new dental technologies for my patients."

Dr. Terry Codington has been a pillar of the dental community for over four decades. After earning his Bachelor of Arts in Biological Sciences from the University of California, Santa Barbara, he went on to complete his Doctor of Dental Surgery (DDS) at the prestigious University of the Pacific School of Dentistry in 1982.

With a career spanning two states, Dr. Codington is licensed to practice in both California and Hawaii. This dual-perspective allows him to bring a unique "Aloha spirit" to his practice in San Mateo, where he combines world-class clinical expertise with a warm, patient-first approach.

Invisalign® Specialist

As a Diamond-level provider, Dr. Codington is recognized for his clinical excellence in clear aligner therapy, helping thousands of patients achieve perfectly aligned smiles.

Sleep Medicine

A member of the AASM, Dr. Codington specializes in non-invasive oral appliance therapy for sleep-disordered breathing and sleep apnea.

As the Principal Dentist of Aloha Dental Group, Dr. Codington remains committed to the intersection of science and art. He is an active leader in the community and is dedicated to providing high-tech solutions that improve not just the oral health, but the overall quality of life for his patients.
